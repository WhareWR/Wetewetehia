Mō Mātou / About Us

He ara hou ki te ako i te reo Māori.
Wetewetehia is a simple, playful tool designed to help you build a daily practice of learning te reo Māori. Our kaupapa is grounded in the belief that language learning should be accessible, enjoyable, and part of a shared journey.

Whether you are just beginning your path with the language or looking to strengthen your existing kete kupu (vocabulary basket), our daily questions offer a quick and engaging way to test your knowledge and watch your understanding grow. Join our community and let's grow our reo together.
